The Mineral Merchant

The Mineral Merchant is a small business I have started that sells crystals, geodes, and other minerals sourced from Brazil. I started this website in the Summer of 2018, and am the sole owner and operator of The Mineral Merchant

FlowField

A simple flow field demo written in JavaScript with the p5.js library. Creates a field of vectors based on Perlin Noise, and creates a number of particles that will follow the noise field.
